Create online polls fast with the Pollar poll maker.

Looking for the best online poll maker? Look no further than the Pollar online poll creator! Whatever the purpose of your poll – be it an opinion poll for research purposes, your general curiosity about people’s thoughts on a particular issue . Pollar is quick, easy and straightforward to use. Get started now!

Get started in 04 simple steps.

  • 01. First start by making your Pollar acount, to create account click on Sign Up on right upper corner.It will take you to registration page.Fill the required details and submit and there you go! you have your Pollar account.

  • 02.To create new poll click on New Poll if you are not logged into your Pollar account it will take you to Log IN page ,after logging in your Pollar account it will take you to page where you can create poll .You can add your options,titile,description and deadline .After creating poll you will get link to share which by clicking people can vote on your poll.

  • 03.To vote on Polls that are created by other users click on Answer Poll .It will take you to page where you can see 10 user's latest Public Polls whose deadline aren't ended.

  • 04.To see the results of polls click on Mypolls .It will take you to page where you can see list of Polls created by you.You can click on poll's title to see that polls result.
